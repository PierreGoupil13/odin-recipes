Chocolat Chaud

The Chocolat Chaud is a well known french recipe which can only be done by french master of Le Chocolat. It has the fine taste of chocolate mixed with the sweetness of the sugar.

Ingredients

  • 100 g of dark chocolate
  • 50 cl of milk
  • 10 cl of water
  • 2 little bags of vanilla sugar

Steps

  1. Break the chocolate in pieces
  2. Heat up the water and milk in a pan
  3. When it boils, add in the vanilla sugar and stur
  4. Finally add in the chocolate and stur again, its ready to be served
