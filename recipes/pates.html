Pâtes au beurre

This is a well konw dish for student with no money at all who still want to eat a bit. It is fat and full of calories while being less than 1 euros to make

Ingredients

  • 200g of pâtes eco
  • A bit of butter
  • That's all

Steps

  1. Boil 500ml of water
  2. When it is boilling, put the pasta in for 6-7 minutes
  3. Serve with a bit of butter
  4. And Voilà
